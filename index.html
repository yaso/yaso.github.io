Hello.

My name is Yasodara Córdova, known as Yaso. I work with technologies to improve the democratic process. Currently, I am a fellow at the Digital Kennedy School at Harvard, where I research Data for public Policies and Digital Identity. Also, I am a Berkman Klein Center affiliated, and a fellow at the Center for Technology & Society (CTS) at FGV School of Law. I also direct the Serenata de Amor Operation

My wide range of interest go from data science to blockchain and cryptocurrencies, and my primary objective is to change the way governments function when they have Internet available. I believe in a world that offers equal opportunities for everyone, based on the human rights principles.
